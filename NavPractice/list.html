Belfast

Co. Antrim

Belfast is the largest and captial city of Northern Ireland.



Belfast is located on the Eastern part of the country famous for its Titanic Museum to discover, Botanic Gardens to relax in the hot sun, Victoria Square for shopping and to learn more about its history and murals.

Belfast

Famous for its historic murals and landmarks, such as the Peace Wall and Titanic Quarter, and for its big-hit movie, Belfast.

Derry City

Co. Derry

Derry City is the second largest city in Northern Ireland.



Derry is located on the North-Western part of the country famous for its Derry Walls (which are the only walls in Europe still in tact), Folyeside and Richmond for shopping and to learn more about the cities history during the Troubles.

Derry City

Famous for its Derry Walls, its historic murals and landmarks, such as Free Derry Corner, and for its hit TV Show, Derry Girls.
